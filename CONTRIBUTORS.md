## AI-Assisted Development (Transparency)

omiiCARE_QA was built with **AI assistance**, and we disclose this openly.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Assistant | Claude (Anthropic) |
| Role | Drafting code, tests, and documentation; scaffolding modules; generating test cases under maintainer direction |
| Oversight | Every AI-assisted change was reviewed, verified, and approved by the maintainer before merge |
| Accountability | The maintainer is solely responsible for all merged content, correctness, and licensing |
| Guardrails | AI usage follows the project's own rules in [docs/AI_DEVELOPMENT_RULES.md](docs/AI_DEVELOPMENT_RULES.md) and [docs/AI_DEVELOPMENT_WORKFLOW.md](docs/AI_DEVELOPMENT_WORKFLOW.md) |
| Data safety | All examples and test data are **synthetic and PHI-safe**; no real patient data was used at any stage |

AI assistance accelerated authoring; it did not replace human judgment. All
decisions, reviews, and the [Definition of Done](docs/DEFINITION_OF_DONE.md)
remained under maintainer control.

## How to Be Listed Here

New contributors are added after their first merged pull request. See
[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) to get started, and
[docs/GITHUB_PROJECT_MANAGEMENT.md](docs/GITHUB_PROJECT_MANAGEMENT.md) for how
work is tracked. Open a `documentation` issue if your contribution merged but
your name is missing.
